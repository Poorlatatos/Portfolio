I spent a significant amount of time working on this project, and I am proud to present it to you. The work done here showcases my skills and dedication to creating high-quality content. I hope you find it engaging and enjoyable.

The project was made in Unity Ver 6000.47f1, and it features a wide variety of assets, including 3D models, textures, and animations.

This was a project that involved two other team members, and we worked together to create a cohesive and immersive experience. Each team member brought their own unique skills and perspectives to the project, which helped to enhance the overall quality of the final product.

The project delves into themes of greed, power, and the consequences of one's actions. This project was a school project, and it was a great opportunity to learn to grow as a developer and work collaboratively with others.
