Introduction

Pandemonium was a project that I developed early on to be a recipe website. I wanted a space where I could share my love for cooking and food, and with that, I combined my web development skills and the onset of having to make a new project for my web development class, to create Pandemonium.

Conception

During the initial development of the porject, I had a difficult time coming up with ways to attract people to the website, and I had a hard time coming up with a design that I liked. I wanted to make a website that was visually appealing, but I also wanted to make a website that was easy to navigate and use. In doing so, I came up with the idea of pairing the webiste with a game where the users can also learn about recipies and cooking whilst playing the game.

Having to make the app itself visually appealing was also a challenge, I went through so many different iterations to get what I exactly wanted.
